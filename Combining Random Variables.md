## $$\mathrm{a±bx}$$
$$
E(a+bx)=a±b·E(x)
$$
$$
Var(a+bx)=b^2\ Var(x)
$$
$$
\sigma_{a+bx}=|b|·\sigma_x
$$
--------------
## $$\mathrm{X±Y}$$
<center>(Make sure X and Y are independent to each other.)</center>

$$
E(X±Y)=E(X)±E(Y)
$$
$$
Var(X±Y)=Var(X)+Var(Y)
$$
$$
\sigma_{X±Y}=\sqrt{{\sigma_X}^2+{\sigma_Y}^2}
$$
------------------
## $$\displaylines{\mathrm{aX+bY}}$$
<center>(independent, a, b are constant)</center>
$$
E(aX+bY)={\color{Red}E(aX)+E(bY)}=aE(X)+bE(Y)
$$
$$
Var(aX+bY)={\color{Red}Var(aX)+Var(bY)}=a^2\ Var(X)+b^2\ Var(Y)
$$
$$
\sigma_{aX+bY}=\sqrt{{a^2\ \sigma_X}^2+b^2\ {\sigma_Y}^2}
$$
