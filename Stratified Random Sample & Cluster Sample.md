## Stratified Random Sample
“Similar within but different between”
在每个stratum里面抽SRS。
#### Advantages
1. Representative
2. Estimate the population more accurately

--------------------------------
#### Disadvantages
1. Consume more time and money


## Cluster Sample
"Different within but similar between"
把SRS的cluster当sample。
#### Advantages
1. Save time and money

-----------------------------------
#### Disadvantages
1. Less representative
2. Cannot offer enough information about the population
